## About
Siramin Adren is a striking figure in Tirion, with an air of regal grace blended seamlessly with the rugged charm of an adventurer. His medium-length, wavy red hair cascades around his shoulders, a stark contrast against his fair elven complexion. Tall and lithe like most elves, Siramin possesses an aura of quiet confidence and wisdom earned from both his upbringing in royalty and his experiences as an adventurer.
