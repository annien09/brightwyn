## About
### Background and Retirement
Gideon is a retired cleric, having once devoted his life to service within a religious order or temple. He served as a healer and counselor, honing his skills in nurturing connections and offering solace to those in need. His retirement from active clerical duties led him to pursue a different path, choosing to channel his passion for hospitality and storytelling into managing The Cleric's Wineskin inn.
