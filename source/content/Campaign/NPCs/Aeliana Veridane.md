## Overview
**Noble Lineage:** 
- Aeliana is a member of the influential Veridane family of Ilayas, daughter of **Lord Cedric Veridane** and **Lady Seraphina Veridane**. Her family is prominent in Ilayas’ political and cultural scenes.
    
**Refined & Enigmatic:** 
- Aeliana is known to be composed, eloquent, and enigmatic in her public appearances. She's often seen at high-profile events such as the Celestial Night Festival and the Veridane Ball.
    
**Fashion Icon:** 
- Her striking beauty and distinctive fashion sense—elegant gowns, elaborate jewelry, and radiant headpieces—have made her a frequent subject of admiration in noble circles.
       
**Well-Read & Cultured:** 
- It’s known she has a strong appreciation for literature and classical arts. She frequents the **Elysian Gallery** and is sometimes seen in the company of bards or scholars.
    
**Unclear Role in Politics:** 
- While she’s a Veridane and expected to play some political role, Aeliana does not hold public office. Still, she seems informed of current events.
- Attends most major events at the behest of the Veridane family.
- Frequently seen speaking with artists, illusionists, and magical scholars rather than military or financial figures.
- Maintains a courteous but distant relationship with nobles who publicly support or oppose her family’s reforms.
