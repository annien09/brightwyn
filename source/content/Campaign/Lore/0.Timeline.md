# Timeline of the Ages

## The Age of Creation  
**–250,000,000 AR to –11,500 AR**

The Age of Creation is a term used to describe the uncounted years of mythic time, beginning with the creation of Brightwyn and ending with the emergence of mortal races upon its face which led to the Age of Legend. It was a time when the deities of Brightwyn played a much more active role than they do today. It was during the Age of Creation that the great battle that led to Rovagug's imprisonment occurred.

---

## The Age of Legend  
**–11,500 AR to –5,292 AR**

The Age of Legend is a general term used to refer to the "prehistoric" time before the destruction of the Starstone led to the Age of Darkness in –5293 AR. It is marked by the rise of the Elven nation, the crossover of the Fey from the First World, the establishment of the first human civilization on Azlant, the Thassilonian Empire, and the rise of dragon rulers in Lung-Wa.

---

## The Age of Darkness  
**–5,292 AR to –4,294 AR**

The Age of Darkness was a thousand-year period in which the sun’s light was blocked by ash from Earthfall. Major civilizations were crippled, especially the human empires of Azlant and Thassilon. Elven realms preserved what lands would later become the heart of modern civilization.

---

## The Age of Anguish  
**–4,294 AR to –3,470 AR**

The Age of Anguish describes roughly 800 years of conflict and survival following Earthfall. The skies cleared, but civilization was fragmented. This age ended with the founding of Osirion and the slow re-establishment of human nations.

---

## The Age of Destiny  
**–3,470 AR to 1 AR**

This age saw the resurgence of human civilization, major wars such as the Nex–Geb War, and culminated in Aroden raising the Starstone and founding Absalom.

---

## The Age of Enthronement  
**1 AR to 4,605 AR**

Marked by mortal ascensions via the Test of the Starstone, including Norgorber, Cayden Cailean, and Iomedae. The Oath Wars and the rise of the Whispering Tyrant shaped the age.

---

## The Age of Glory (Unfulfilled)  
**4,605 AR to 4,605 AR**

Prophesied to be a golden age, it never came. Aroden failed to return, divine magic faltered, and the world fell into chaos.

---

## The Age of Lost Omens  
**4,606 AR to Present**

The current age, defined by uncertainty, broken prophecy, planar instability, and political upheaval following Aroden’s death.
